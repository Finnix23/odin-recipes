Pancakes

Description

A easy and tasty recipe which you can make in under 10 minutes. The recipe is for four pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. Whisk together the flour ant the eggs. Gradually add in the milk and water, stirring to combine. Add the salt and butter; beat until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  3. Cook the pancakes for about 2 minutes each, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side.
  4. Enjoy your meal
